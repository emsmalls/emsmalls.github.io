BEEP

Over the duration of Summer 2016, I worked on a project with 5 fellow students at UCSD to design, test and implement a parking app which would effectively solve UCSD's parking dilemma. We created a mobile platform with features including real time analytics, GPS location to remember your car location, the option to remotely purchase parking permits and lot chat.

  • Client: UCSD
  • Role: Lead User Research, Project Management, Marketing
  • Year: 2016

COGS 102C: Greening UCSD

A project based design course. The Green team conducted user research and designed a mandatory environmental education course for entering freshman students.

Greening UCSD

During Spring 2016, I worked on a team with 5 fellow cognitive science students to devise a plan to increase environmental awareness at UCSD. We began conducting interviews with our needer group, UCSD students, and found the majority did not attain even basic knowledge of the reprecussions of not recycling. Upon researching current "green" proposals for the school, we pitched a mandatory environmental education course for incoming freshman.

  • Client: UCSD
  • Role: User Experience Research, Needfinding, Rapid Prototyping
  • Year: 2016
